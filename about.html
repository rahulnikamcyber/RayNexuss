Our Story

Founded in 2022, Perplexity AI emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to make the world's knowledge accessible to everyone through natural language conversations. Our team of researchers, engineers, and visionaries came together to create an AI that doesn't just provide answers, but understands context, verifies information, and learns from every interaction.

What started as a research project has evolved into a comprehensive AI platform that serves millions of users worldwide, helping them discover, learn, and make informed decisions faster than ever before.
